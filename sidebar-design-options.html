Design Recommendations Summary

Option 1: Minimal Pills

Best for: Clean, professional look. Uses familiar patterns. Easy to implement. Recommended if staying with light theme.

Option 2: Dark Cards

Best for: Modern SaaS aesthetic. Great for data-heavy apps. May require theme toggle. Good for power users.

Option 3: Compact Segmented

Best for: Limited space. Multiple view modes. Users who switch contexts often. Most information-dense.
